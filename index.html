Set up your
Tronlink wallet

Once you set up your wallet, connect it to NFTone by clicking the Log in button in the top right corner of the website. Then follow the instructions. Learn more about how to set up a Tronlink wallet in our FAQ.

Fill in
your profile

Open your profile page and click the Edit profile button in the profile. Add your name and links to your profiles on social media. You may also provide a short description of yours and add your profile and banner images.

Be a
publisher

Click Be a Publisher. Upload a few samples of your artworks, provide your name, short bio and email and click Submit. Once our team verifies your status you’ll be able to create NFTs. Learn more about the costs in our FAQ.

List your
NFTs for sale

Upload an image, a video or 3D art. Add its title, description and collection name. Set up an auction and click Create. Voila! Your NFT is created on the Tron blockchain and ready for selling on NFTone. All data is stored on BTFS.
